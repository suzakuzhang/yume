# yume Knowledge Base — Master Index

> A guidance-oriented dream journal. The same daily ritual runs every day: a user offers one **dream IMAGERY element**, it is reverse-guided into a generated image rendered in **Chinese-painting-appreciation (画作赏析) brushstrokes**, then **four interpretive lenses become debating AGENTS** who each establish a view and then 求同存异 (seek-common-ground-while-preserving-difference). Dream-science and philosophy sit underneath as **grounding / referee** layers. Over a timeline the same machinery enables **纵向 (longitudinal)** reading of the dreamer's individuation.

## 2. The four interpretive lenses (the PRIMARY debaters)

| Lens | Core claim about the dream | Method | Stance toward divination |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Freud — psychoanalytic** | A *disguised wish-fulfilment*; manifest content hides latent dream-thoughts produced by the dream-work (condensation, displacement, representability, secondary revision). | Free association; undo the dream-work; fixed (largely sexual/Oedipal) symbol catalogue; trace the day's residue. | Hostile — treats oracles as resistance/rationalization. |
| **Jung — analytical psychology** | A *truthful self-portrait* of the psyche; **compensates** a one-sided conscious attitude; teleological, points forward. | Amplification (stay with the image, enrich with myth); subjective vs. objective level; read in series; flag "big dreams". | The strongest bridge — via **synchronicity** a dream, a cast hexagram, and a drawn card are co-expressions of one constellated archetype. |
| **Chinese 周公解梦 / oneiromancy** | An *omen and a moral-cosmic signal* (梦为兆): the dream is a sign of 吉/凶 read against fortune, family, health, and the yin-yang / five-agent order — but only *after* classifying the dream type (六梦 / 王符十梦), so 想梦/病梦 are not forced into prophecy. | 辨类而后断: classify the dream (六梦/十梦) → judge 真伪 (直梦 vs 想梦/病梦/反梦) → 取象断吉凶 via the 解梦 lexicon, 谐音, and 阴阳五行类象; weigh by dreamer's 身份/时令; offer 梦禳 (ritual relief) for nightmares. | Native kin to 起卦 — shares the same 阴阳五行 / 八卦类象 cosmological grammar; supplies the 梦象起卦 接口. |
| **Divination — 周易起卦 + Tarot 抽塔罗** | The dream is *a draw from the same deck as the cards*: meaningful by **correspondence and resonance** (as above, so below), not by cause. | Map images to Tarot trump + planet + element + Tree-of-Life altitude + hexagram; locate on the Fool's Journey / alchemical arc; ask "why this image now?" | Is itself the divinatory layer. |

### Supporting layers (GROUNDING / REFEREE — they do not decode)

- **Dream science (`06_science.md`) — GROUNDING.** Keeps the debaters empirically honest. Supplies: **threat-simulation theory** (Revonsuo 2000 — dreams over-rehearse threats, so chase/danger dreams are expected, not prophetic); **sleep-dependent memory consolidation** (Stickgold; Wamsley — dreaming of recent tasks aids consolidation); **activation-synthesis** (Hobson & McCarley 1977 — bizarreness is brain self-organization, not disguise) and the AIM/Solms forebrain refinements; the **continuity hypothesis** (Hall–Van de Castle; Domhoff — dream content statistically mirrors waking concerns, the most app-relevant and best-validated principle); plus the posterior "hot zone" (Siclari 2017), DMN-as-dreaming (Domhoff & Fox 2015), and lucid-dream science (Voss 2009; Konkoly 2021). It may **veto** a falsified mechanism claim (e.g. teeth-loss as possible bruxism/sensation before any "message"; falling/flying as possible vestibular reactivation) but never dictates meaning. Typical-dream prevalences (chased ~80%, falling ~74%, flying ~50%, teeth ~19%; Nielsen) feed the longitudinal layer.
- **Philosophy of dreams (`07_philosophy.md`) — the REFEREE.** Brackets the meaning question and audits *status and certainty*: Is this perception, hallucination, or imagining (Windt's ISTH; Ichikawa's imagination model; Revonsuo/Metzinger simulation)? How do we know waking is more real (Descartes' dream argument; Zhuangzi's 物化 / butterfly)? The report is not the dream (Windt/Dennett report-humility). Both dream and waking are world-models (Metzinger; Vedanta's four states + turīya witness; dream yoga lucidity). It also speaks **Sartre/Bachelard** on yume's imagery-to-image branch: the generated image is an *analogon* that should aim for **reverberation**, not literal illustration. It can force every other agent to re-state confidence.

## 3. The post-Freudian toolkit (`03_post_freudian.md`)

Not a single debater but a **pluralist reservoir** the agents draw on, plus the engine of the longitudinal layer:

- **Adler** — teleological "rehearsal" reading: the dream rehearses the *style of life* and manufactures the mood that justifies the dreamer's chosen line (watch for self-deceptive exaggeration of power).
- **Hillman** — "befriend the image, do not translate it"; a "stay with the image" mode with no interpret button.
- **Boss (Daseinsanalysis)** — phenomenological: what *world* is disclosed, how open/closed/attuned is the dreamer's existence (sibling to the philosophy referee).
- **Lacan** — the unconscious "structured like a language"; track puns, repeated words, homophones, and "who is this addressed to?".
- **Gestalt/Perls + Faraday** — every element is a disowned part of the self; "become each part" (empty-chair) role-play; reject dream dictionaries.
- **Hall–Van de Castle** — quantitative content coding (characters, interactions, emotions, settings, misfortunes) scored against norms; the **neutral statistical backbone** of the 纵向 timeline, shared with `06_science.md`.
- **Hartmann** — Central Image (CI) + its intensity track the dominant emotion; thin/thick boundaries; nightmare rescripting (IRT — *attribute to Krakow et al., not Hartmann*).

## 4. How the lenses relate

```
            PHILOSOPHY  (referee: audits status, certainty, report-humility)
                 │
   ┌─────────────┼───────────────────────────────────┐
 FREUD   ┄┄┄┄┄  JUNG  ┄┄(synchronicity bridge)┄┄  DIVINATION (易+Tarot)
 (backward,    (forward,                          (correspondence,
  disguise,     compensation,                      Fool's Journey,
  sexual)       archetypes)                        alchemical arc)
       │         │                                       │
       │         └──────────  周公解梦  ─────────────────┘
       │              (omen / 吉凶 / 八卦类象, shares 起卦 vocabulary)
       │                          │
       └────  POST-FREUDIAN  ─────┤  (Adler/Hillman/Boss/Lacan/Gestalt/HVdC/Hartmann)
                                  │
            DREAM SCIENCE  (grounding: mechanism, function, continuity)
```

- **Freud ↔ Jung**: agree the dream is meaningful and image-based; clash on *disguise vs. truthful self-portrait*, *backward/sexual vs. forward/archetypal*, *free association vs. amplification*.
- **Freud ↔ 周公解梦**: the sharpest opposition. Freud reads the dream **backward** to an internal *past wish* via free association and a fixed (sexual) symbol catalogue; 周公 reads it **forward** as an external *future omen* via fixed-lexicon lookup and 谐音/类象. Same image, opposite vector and opposite locus (inner desire vs. outer fate).
- **Jung ↔ Divination ↔ 周公**: Jung is the bridge. Via **synchronicity** a dream, a cast hexagram, and a drawn card are co-expressions of one constellated archetype; the alchemical arc (nigredo/albedo/rubedo ≈ Shadow/purification/Self) aligns with the Fool's Journey. Jung and 周公 *look* alike (both symbolic — snake, water, dragon) but differ: 周公's 象义 is culturally fixed and adjudicates 吉/凶; Jung's is individual, integrative, and indifferent to fortune.
- **Divination ↔ 周公解梦**: share a correspondence cosmology, and 04's **梦象起卦** explicitly maps a dream's main image to **八卦类象** (火→离, 水→坎, 龙/雷→震, 山/止→艮 …) to cast a hexagram — so the Chinese lens and the 起卦 module **share one 类象/阴阳五行 vocabulary**. They differ in that 周公解梦 is indigenous Chinese and morally/fortune-oriented, while the Tarot side of the divination lens is here a *Western-received* (Wilhelm/Baynes + Jung) reading — **yume must keep these distinct and not flatten the indigenous tradition**.
- **Science ↔ all decoders**: grounds, normalizes, and may veto — chase dreams are common rehearsal (threat-simulation), bizarreness is synthesis, recurrent themes mirror waking concerns (continuity).
- **Philosophy ↔ all**: refuses verdicts, exposes smuggled metaphysics, bounds everyone's confidence. Pair it with a warmer lens so its rigor sharpens rather than freezes the reading.

## 5. Source-hygiene rules (load-bearing)

- **Label scholarly vs. practitioner sources.** Historians (Yates, Hanegraaff) are cited for *provenance only* and do **not** endorse the systems' truth-claims; practitioners (Waite, Crowley, Fortune, Freud, Jung) supply the working symbol grammar. Empirical science (06) and philosophy (07) are cited as fact/argument respectively. Never blur the classes.
- **Public-domain full text only where verified.** Freud: Brill 1900/1913 (Gutenberg #66048), Hubback 1922 *Beyond the Pleasure Principle*. Jung: Hinkle 1916 (Gutenberg #65903). Chinese: 维基文库 for 周礼/潜夫论/列子/庄子/灵枢, Internet Archive scans for 梦占逸旨/梦林玄解, P.3908 敦煌本 via 《敦煌宝藏》. Tarot: Waite *Pictorial Key* (sacred-texts). I Ching: Legge 1899 PD text (sacred-texts) as the open alternative to the in-copyright Wilhelm/Baynes. Science: open-access PMC/lab reprints (Walker & van der Helm 2009; van der Helm 2011; Domhoff & Fox 2015; Voss 2009; Konkoly 2021) + Hall/Van de Castle coding rules (dreams.ucsc.edu); core neuroscience papers (Aserinsky/Kleitman, Hobson/McCarley, Solms, Revonsuo, Stickgold, Siclari) are paywalled — cite, do not reproduce. Philosophy: Descartes (use English PD, Wikisource/Gutenberg #70091, **not** the Latin #23306), Zhuangzi (ctext Legge), Aristotle (classics.mit.edu), Māṇḍūkya (sacred-texts sbe15), SEP "Dreams and Dreaming" (open access).
- **Known corrections carried in `GAPS.md`.** Several dossier URLs are dead or mislabeled (Freud "On Dreams"/"Symbolism" copyright slips; Jung I Ching ISBN 404 and two over-optimistic "open_access" labels; dead 777 archive ID; Emerald-Tablet-vs-Corpus-Hermeticum conflation; Aserinsky/Kleitman wrong PMID; Revonsuo PhilPapers slug; IRT misattributed to Hartmann; three Philosophy full-text 404/mislabels). Honor these before serving any full text.
